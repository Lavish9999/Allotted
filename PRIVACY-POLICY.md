# Privacy Policy — Allotted

_Last updated: June 24, 2026_

Allotted is built to keep your financial information private. This policy explains, in plain language, what the app does and does not do with your data.

## The short version

Allotted does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any of your data. Everything you enter stays on your device.

## What data the app handles

When you use Allotted, you enter information such as income amounts, budget categories, bills, debts, planned amounts, settings, backups, and individual transactions. All of this is stored **locally on your device** using your device's standard on-device storage.

## What we do with it

Nothing. The app has no servers, no accounts, and no analytics. Your data is never sent anywhere. The developer cannot see it, and no third party receives it.

## Network use

Allotted does not make network requests in the course of normal use. It works fully offline.

## Backups

Allotted lets you create a backup of your data, which you can copy to your clipboard or save as a file, and restore later. These backups are created and stored by you, wherever you choose to put them (for example, your Notes, email, Files app, or cloud storage). The developer has no access to them.

## Children's privacy

Allotted does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children.

## Data deletion

Because your data lives only on your device, you can delete all of it at any time using the "Start over" option in the app's settings, by clearing app/browser data, or by deleting the app.

## Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted at this page with a new "Last updated" date.
