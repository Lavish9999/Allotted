## Description  *(max 4000 chars)*
```
Allotted is a simple, private way to plan your money one month at a time.

The idea is straightforward: at the start of each month, give every dollar of income a job — bills, groceries, savings, fun — until nothing is left unassigned. Then as the month goes on, log what you spend and watch each category drain down. You always know exactly where you stand.

No bank logins. No syncing. No accounts. Everything you enter stays on your device, and only your device.

WHAT YOU CAN DO
• Build a monthly plan and assign every dollar to a category
• Track spending against your plan and see what's left in real time
• Carry last month's plan forward in one tap
• Review the year at a glance
• Back up and restore your data whenever you want

WHY ALLOTTED
• Private by design — your numbers never leave your phone
• Fast and quiet — no ads, no notifications nagging you, no upsells
• Built for clarity — a clean, focused view of your month

Allotted doesn't connect to your bank or pull transactions automatically. That's the point: you decide where your money goes, on purpose, before you spend it.
```

## App Privacy "Nutrition Label" answers (App Store Connect questionnaire)
When App Store Connect asks "Do you or your third-party partners collect data from this app?":

**Answer: No, we do not collect data from this app.**

This is accurate because Allotted stores everything in local on-device storage, has no analytics, no network calls, and no third-party SDKs. This single answer produces the "Data Not Collected" label — the cleanest possible privacy badge.
