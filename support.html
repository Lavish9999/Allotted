How does Allotted work?

At the start of each month, you give every dollar of your income a job across bills, groceries, savings, debt, and other spending. Then you log spending and watch each category draw down.

Where is my data stored?

Only on your device. Allotted has no accounts and no servers, and your information never leaves your phone during normal app use.

How do I back up my data?

Open Settings and use "Copy private backup" or "Download backup file." Store that backup somewhere private and safe. Backup files contain your budget, bills, debts, and transactions.

Can you recover my data if I lose it?

No. Since nothing is stored on a server, the only way to restore lost data is from a backup you created.

Is Allotted free?

Yes. No ads, no subscriptions, no in-app purchases, and no tracking.
