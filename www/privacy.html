Allotted Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 24, 2026

Short version: Allotted is designed to keep your budgeting data on your device. The app does not require an account, does not connect to your bank, does not run ads, and does not sell your data.

Data You Enter

Allotted stores the budgets, bills, debts, transactions, settings, and backups you create locally on your device using app/browser storage.

Data Collection

Allotted does not collect personal information through normal app use. There is no account system, analytics SDK, advertising SDK, or bank-login connection in the app.

Backups

If you export, copy, download, paste, email, or store an Allotted backup, that backup may contain sensitive financial information. You control where those backup files or copied text go.

Network Use

The core app is built to work locally. If you contact support or visit a support page, your browser or email provider may process that separate communication.

Deleting Data

If you delete the app, clear app/browser data, or reset Allotted without a backup, your local budgeting history may be permanently lost.

Contact

For privacy questions, use the support contact listed on the Allotted support page.
