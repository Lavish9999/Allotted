Allotted Support

Allotted is a private, local-first budgeting app for giving every dollar a job.

Before deleting the app: create a backup from Settings. Allotted stores your budget locally, so deleting app data without a backup can remove your history.

Common Help

  • Use Plan to assign income to savings, bills, and spending categories.
  • Use Spend to log purchases and see what is left.
  • Use Bills for recurring monthly payments and due dates.
  • Use More for debt payoff, weekly review, year view, settings, privacy, and backups.

Backups

Go to Settings and create a backup regularly. Store it somewhere you trust, such as Files, iCloud Drive, or another private location.
