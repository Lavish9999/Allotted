How does Allotted work?

At the start of each month, you give every dollar of your income a job — across categories like bills, groceries, and savings — until nothing's left unassigned. Then you log what you spend and watch each category draw down. It's the zero-based budgeting method.

Where is my data stored?

Only on your device. Allotted has no accounts and no servers, and your information never leaves your phone.

How do I back up my data?

Open Settings (the gear icon) and use "Copy backup to clipboard" or "Download backup file." Paste or save it somewhere safe. Because your data is device-only, regular backups are important — if you delete the app or clear your browser without one, the data can't be recovered.

Can you recover my data if I lose it?

No. Since nothing is stored on any server, the only way to restore lost data is from a backup you made. Please back up regularly.

Is Allotted really free?

Yes — no ads, no subscriptions, no in-app purchases, and no tracking.
